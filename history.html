The History of Concentrix

From a single subsidiary to a global leader in customer experience.

Key Milestones

1983

Concentrix is originally founded, beginning its long journey in the customer engagement industry.

2006

SYNNEX Corporation acquires Concentrix, setting the stage for massive global expansion and technological integration.

2014

Concentrix acquires the IBM Customer Care business (IBM CRM), significantly expanding its global footprint and services.

2018

Concentrix acquires Convergys, making it the second-largest global provider of customer engagement services.

2020

Concentrix spins off from SYNNEX and becomes an independent, publicly traded company on the NASDAQ (CNXC).

2023

Concentrix completes a highly anticipated merger with Webhelp, further expanding its dominance in the CX space worldwide.

Visionary Leadership

Robert Huang

Founder of SYNNEX Corporation, the parent company that originally nurtured and scaled Concentrix into an industry powerhouse.

Chris Caldwell

President and CEO of Concentrix. He led the company through its biggest acquisitions, public spin-off, and explosive global growth.
